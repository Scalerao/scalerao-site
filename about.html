Our Story

From Operators. For Operators.

ScaleRao started in 2020 with a simple thesis: most Amazon and Flipkart agencies are still running playbooks from 2015. They move slowly, use generic rules, and charge a percentage of spend regardless of whether they actually moved the needle.

We built ScaleRao differently. Every account that comes in gets paired with a senior marketplace operator and — from day one — connected to OrionAI, our proprietary decision engine. OrionAI runs bid intelligence, keyword harvesting, TACoS tracking and self-healing automation every four hours, on every account, without humans being the bottleneck.

Humans still drive strategy: positioning, creative, account health cases, category tactics. But the mechanical work — the part that used to require a team of ten analysts and still missed things — runs on code that doesn't sleep, doesn't quit, and gets smarter per-account over time.

Today we manage 1,000+ active campaigns and ₹2Cr+ in ad spend annually across Amazon India and Flipkart, with 98% client retention. We operate as BuyBox on record (the legal entity), doing business as ScaleRao. Headquartered in New Delhi.

How We Work

Four Principles. Non-Negotiable.

01

Data Over Opinions

Every bid, every keyword, every listing change — traced to data. We don't guess and we don't pretend we know things we don't.

02

TACoS, Not ACoS

Short-term ad efficiency is vanity. We manage total ad cost of sales against organic growth — the only metric that compounds.

03

Own It Fully

Advertising without account health is futile. We own the whole surface — ads, listings, compliance, FBA, P&L — end to end.

04

Client's Money First

Ad spend is billed directly by platforms — never through us. No markup, no hidden margin. Our fee is our fee. Aligned incentives.
