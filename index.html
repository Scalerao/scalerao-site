What is TACoS and why is it more important than ACoS?
ACoS only measures ad spend against ad-attributed revenue. TACoS measures ad spend against your total revenue — organic + paid. A falling TACoS means your ads are building organic rank and you're growing less dependent on paid traffic. ScaleRao tracks TACoS weekly and uses it to guide bid strategy — scaling when healthy, cutting waste when high — to build long-term profitable growth.
How does Amazon's search algorithm decide who ranks #1?
Amazon's search algorithm ranks products based on multiple signals: sales velocity, conversion rate, listing relevance (title, bullets, backend keywords), click-through rate, customer review quality, price competitiveness, and fulfillment reliability. It also understands the semantic intent behind searches — not just exact keyword matches. We optimize all these signals systematically, not just run ads in isolation.
My listings are getting suppressed — what's happening?
Listing suppression happens due to policy violations: prohibited characters (| ! $ ™ ©), word repetition beyond the allowed limit, warranty or guarantee claims in bullets, missing required attributes, or inaccurate product information. Amazon suppresses search visibility or auto-corrects your title without warning. ScaleRao performs a full compliance audit to identify and fix all violations — often recovering listings within 24-48 hours.
How quickly will I see results?
ACoS improvements typically appear within 30-45 days of campaign restructuring. Conversion rate improvements from listing optimization are often visible in 2-3 weeks. Organic rank growth from TACoS management takes 60-90 days as Amazon's algorithm registers consistent sales patterns. We give you a realistic timeline after the free audit based on your specific account situation.
Do you manage Flipkart accounts too?
Yes. ScaleRao manages both Amazon and Flipkart seller accounts. Each platform has different ranking signals, ad formats, and content guidelines — we apply platform-specific strategies for each. Flipkart advertising, catalog management, and account health are handled with the same rigorous approach as Amazon.
What is AdsPilot and how does it benefit me as a client?
AdsPilot is ScaleRao's proprietary AI optimization system — custom built, not an off-the-shelf tool. It runs twice daily, analyzing your campaign data, processing Search Term Reports, harvesting winning keywords, negating waste, and adjusting bids based on TACoS, ROAS, and ACoS targets — automatically. For you, this means your account is optimized even at 3 AM, with zero delay between data and action. It also generates automated monthly P&L reports so you always know your exact financial performance.
What reporting do I get?
Monthly P&L reports covering ad spend, total revenue, organic vs paid split, Amazon fees, net margins, ACoS, TACoS, and ROAS — with category and ASIN-level breakdowns. Weekly performance updates via WhatsApp. You retain full access to your Seller Central at all times. All data is yours, always.
